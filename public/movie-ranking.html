The best anime according to Vangelis

My top 3 anime of all time.

1. Steins:Gate

Time travel, intricate conspiracies and love transcending time and space - the ultimate blend!

2. Monster

Fantastic anime mixing a detective story with redemption, revenge and a deep exploration of the human psyche. Worth every minute.

3. Code Geass

A futuristic Shakespearean tale about hunger for power and what one is willing to throw away to achieve their dream.
